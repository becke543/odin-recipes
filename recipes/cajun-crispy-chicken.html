Cajun Crispy Chicken

The crispy cajun chicken sandwich is tempura battered chicken served with pickle, spicy mayo on a brioche bun and seasoned with Cajun spices.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken tenderloins
  • tempura batter mix
  • olive oil
  • brioche buns
  • mayo
  • cajun seasoning

Steps:

  1. Heat oil over medium heat pan
  2. Dip chicken tenderloins and cover completely in tempura batter mix
  3. Once oil is sizzling, drop tenderloins into oil. Wait until crisp, then remove and place on paper towel
  4. Toast brioche buns
  5. Layer buns with mayo, chicken tenderloins, dill pickles. Ready to eat.
